About Me

I was born in Mexico City, Mexico. I received my BA in Linguistics at the Universidad Autónoma Metropolitana, Iztapalapa campus. In 2015, I traveled to Canada to study my MA at the University of Toronto, where I am currently studying the Ph.D.

My passion for linguistics can only be compared to my love for writing, practicing martial arts and playing music with friends. When my graduate student life allows it, I divide my time between writing short stories, poems and songs, practicing Shaolin quan and Wing Chun, and playing saxophone, jarana and —most recently— the bongos.
