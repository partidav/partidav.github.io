Hi! I am Virgilio Partida-Peñalva, a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Toronto.

I work on theoretical syntax and morphology. My research is focused on the agreement system of Mazahua, an Oto-Manguean language spoken in Central Mexico.
